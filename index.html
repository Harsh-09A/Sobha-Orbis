SOBHA ORBIS

Discover SOBHA ORBIS with its thoughtfully designed apartments in 1-2 bedroom configurations. The complex is being developed by Sobha Realty and is situated in Motor City, famous for the Dubai Autodrome. Sobha Realty Redefines The Concept Of Luxury Residential Living With A Repertoire Of Real Estate Projects Across Dubai. Pioneering The 'Backward Integration' Model In Real Estate, Sobha Realty Employs In-House Mastery To Create A Unique Offering Of Fine Living That Extends Beyond Customer's Expectations.

SOBHA ORBIS is a premium 3-tower complex by Sobha Realty in the coveted area of Motor City. It houses a collection of 1-2 bedroom apartments, including 1.5-bedroom configurations, ranging from 545 sq.ft. to 988 sq.ft. Investors also have the option to purchase a full floor in the Sobha Orbis complex. Key features of the layouts include a utility room, a storage room, a powder room and a balcony. Please note that 1.5-bedroom apartments come with an office/study, a major benefit considering the ongoing trend of working and studying remotely. The complex offers its occupants an array of unrivalled leisure amenities, including a resort-style pool, landscaped gardens, a fitness centre and more.

About Location

SOBHA ORBIS offers easy access to Mohammed bin Zayed Road and Al Qudra Road, ensuring seamless connectivity with the rest of Dubai. As a result, Dubai International Airport, Business Bay and Downtown Dubai can be reached within a radius of 30 minutes by car.

Those who prefer public transport can take advantage of several bus stations nearby, including Motor City/Uptown Appartments 1 and Motor City/Uptown Appartments 2.

Within walking distance of SOBHA ORBIS are many social infrastructure facilities, such as the following supermarkets, hospitals and nurseries: Spinneys, Emirates Hospital Day Surgery & Medical Center, Motor City, Dubai, Jebel Ali Village Nursery and GEMS Metropole School.
